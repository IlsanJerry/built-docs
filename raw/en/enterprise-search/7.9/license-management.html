License management

Workplace Search is available under a basic license. You can use many of its feature for free!

However, some features are gated under a Platinum subscription.

This means that the underlying Elasticsearch clusters require a Platinum license.

You can activate a 30 day trial or purchase a Platinum license.

Starting a trial

A 30 day Platinum license trial can be applied to Elasticsearch.

Once applied, you will be able to experience the full feature set of Workplace Search.

There are two methods of applying trial to Elasticsearch:

  1. Through the Kibana UI
  2. Through the Elasticsearch API

If you need to revert back to a Basic license, you can use the Elasticsearch Revert to Basic API.
