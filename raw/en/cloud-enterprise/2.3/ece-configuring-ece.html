Configuring your installation

Now that you have Elastic Cloud Enterprise up and running, take a look at some of the additional features that you can configure:

  • System clusters configuration - Best practices for ECE system clusters to ensure a highly available and resilient setup.
  • Configure deployment templates - Make the most out of deployment templates by configuring ECE for your hardware and creating custom deployment templates.
  • Manage snapshot repositories - To back up your Elasticsearch clusters automatically, you need to configure a snapshot repository.
  • Manage licenses - Keep Elastic Cloud Enterprise current with a valid license.
  • Change endpoint URLs - Set where Elasticsearch and Kibana can be accessed from.
