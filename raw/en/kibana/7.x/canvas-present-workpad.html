When you are ready to present your workpad, use and enable the presentation options.

View your workpad in fullscreen modeedit

Enable autoplayedit

Automatically cycle through your workpads pages in fullscreen mode.

  1. In the upper left corner, click the Control settings icon.

  2. Under Change cycling interval, select the interval you want to use.
