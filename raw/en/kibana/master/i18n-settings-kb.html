i18n settings in Kibanaedit

You do not need to configure any settings to run Kibana in English.

General i18n Settingsedit

i18n.locale

Kibana currently supports the following locales:

  • English - en (default)
  • Chinese - zh-CN
  • Japanese - ja-JP
