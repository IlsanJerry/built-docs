Add sample data

Kibana has several sample data sets that you can use to explore Kibana before loading your own data. These sample data sets showcase a variety of use cases:

  • eCommerce orders includes visualizations for product-related information, such as cost, revenue, and price.
  • Flight data enables you to view and interact with flight routes.
  • Web logs lets you analyze website traffic.

To get started, go to the Kibana home page and click the link underneath Add sample data.

Once you’ve loaded a data set, click View data to view prepackaged visualizations, dashboards, Canvas workpads, Maps, and Machine Learning jobs.

The timestamps in the sample data sets are relative to when they are installed. If you uninstall and reinstall a data set, the timestamps will change to reflect the most recent installation.
