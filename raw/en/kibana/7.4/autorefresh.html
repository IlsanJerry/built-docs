Refresh the search resultsedit

As more documents are added to the indices you’re searching, the search results shown in Discover and used to display visualizations get stale. You can configure a refresh interval to periodically resubmit your searches to retrieve the latest results.

  1. Click .
  2. In the Refresh every field, enter the refresh rate, then select the interval from the dropdown.

  3. Click Start.

To disable auto refresh, click Stop.

If auto refresh is not enabled, click Refresh to manually refresh the search results.
