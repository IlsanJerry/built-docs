Making Schezwan sauce

This is an example of a stub you can add to this page.

To make Schezwan sauce:

  1. Step 1: Look up the spelling of Schezwan. Some spell it Sichuan, others Szechuan.
  2. Step 2: brew install schezwan-sauce
  3. Step 3: sudo make sauce
