Service status

Elasticsearch Service is a hosted service for the Elastic Stack that runs on different cloud platforms, such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). Like any service, it might undergo availability changes from time to time. When availability changes, we do our best to provide you with a current service status.

To see current and past service availability, go to our Cloud Status page.

Subscribe to updates

Don’t want to check our service status page manually? We can notify you about changes to the status of our service automatically.

To receive service status updates:

  1. Go to our Cloud Status page and click SUBSCRIBE TO UPDATES.

  2. Select the method for getting notified of status updates. We support:

    • Email
    • Twitter
    • Atom and RSS feeds

After you subscribe to updates, you will receive a notification whenever we post a service status update.
