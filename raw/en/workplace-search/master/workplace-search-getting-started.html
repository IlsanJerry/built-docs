Getting started with Workplace Searchedit

Welcome to Workplace Search!

It provides enjoyable, intuitive, high volume search across your entire organization.

It’s a fast, scalable, and relevant search bar for your everyday work life. And it’s all Elastic under the hood.

This introduction assumes you have logged in and are looking at a fresh administration page.

If you aren’t running yet, read the Installing page for instructions.

There are a few key steps needed to get rolling:

  1. Content sources overview
  2. Administrative tools guide
  3. Hand out The searcher’s manual to teach your team mates The Ways of Search.

If you need help or have beta feedback, please email us!
