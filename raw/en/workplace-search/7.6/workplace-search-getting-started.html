Getting Started with Workplace Search

Welcome to Workplace Search!

Workplace Search provides a fast, scalable, and relevant search experience for your everyday work life, across your entire organization. And it’s all Elastic under the hood.

This introduction assumes you have logged in and are looking at a fresh administration page. If you aren’t up and running yet, read the Installing guide for detailed instructions.

There are a few key steps needed to get rolling:

  1. Content sources overview
  2. Administrative tools guide
  3. Hand out The searcher’s manual to teach your team mates The Ways of Search.

Workplace Search beta feedback is always welcome and appreciated. Don’t hesitate to send us an email!
