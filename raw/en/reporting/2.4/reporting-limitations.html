Limitations

High Report Request Loads Can Result in Watch Notification Failures

If the interval between report requests is shorter than the time it takes to generate the reports, the report queue can back up. If you are using Watcher to automate report generation, this can result in notification failures.

To keep the report queue from backing up, stagger your report requests or increase the trigger interval for your watches that generate reports.

Blank Reports Generated

If your generated reports contain blank charts, try increasing the reporting.capture.loadDelay setting in kibana.yml.
