License Management

When you initially install X-Pack, a 30 day trial license is installed that allows access to all features. At the end of the trial period, you can purchase a subscription to keep using the full functionality of the X-Pack components.

When your license expires, X-Pack operates in a degraded mode. For more information, see License Expiration.
