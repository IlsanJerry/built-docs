Prebuilt job referenceedit

Prebuilt jobs automatically detect file system and network anomalies on your hosts. To enable jobs when you are not using Beats, you must map your data to the ECS fields listed in each job description.

Some jobs use fields that are not ECS-compliant. These jobs are only available when you use Beats to ship data.
