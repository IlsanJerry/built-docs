Benchmark rulesedit

The Benchmark Integrations page lets you view the cloud security posture (CSP) benchmark rules for your Kubernetes security posture management (KSPM) integrations. Benchmark rules define the Kubernetes configuration best practices that form the basis of the data that appears on the Cloud Posture dashboard.

To find the Benchmark Integrations page, go to Manage → CSP Benchmarks. From there, to view the benchmark rules associated with an integration, select that integration’s name.

You can then click on a benchmark rule’s name to see details, including information about how to remediate failures and related links.

Benchmark rules are not editable.
