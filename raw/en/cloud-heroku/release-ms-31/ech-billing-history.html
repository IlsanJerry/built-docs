See your billing history

Information about outstanding payments and billing receipts is available from the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console.

To see your billing history:

  1. Sign in to the Elasticsearch Add-On for Heroku console.
  2. Select your deployment from the Elastic Cloud portal.
  3. Go to Account and then Billing history.
  4. Click on the invoice number for a detailed PDF.
