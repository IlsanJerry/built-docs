This list summarizes the most important enhancements in Kibana 8.4.

Enhancements to Alertingedit

Add schedules for snoozing rule notificationsedit

Starting in 8.2, you could suppress the notifications and actions for your rules for a specific duration. Now you can also schedule these single or recurring downtimes to start and end at specific dates and times.
