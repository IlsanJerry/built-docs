Kibana highlightsedit

This list summarizes the most important enhancements in Kibana 8.4.

Coming in 8.4.0.

Enhancements to Alertingedit

Webhook - Case Management connectoredit

The new Webhook - Case Management connector and action enable you to send POST, PUT, and GET requests to a case management RESTful API web service. You can use this connector with cases in Observability, Stack Management, and the Elastic Security app.

Add schedules for snoozing rule notificationsedit

Starting in 8.2, you could suppress the notifications and actions for your rules for a specific duration. Now you can also schedule these single or recurring downtimes to start and end at specific dates and times.

New metrics for rules and actionsedit

You can now ship metrics related to alerting features to your monitoring cluster by using the Elastic Agent or Metricbeat. Click Overview and Instances in the Kibana section of Stack Monitoring to see visualizations about the rules and actions that are queued, running, or failing.
