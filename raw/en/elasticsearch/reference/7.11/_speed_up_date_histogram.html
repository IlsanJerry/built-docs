Speed up Date histogramedit

In 7.10 we managed an 11% speed improvement in Date histogram. In 7.11 we’ve sped it up an additional 85% in some common cases - most noteably when the date_histogram is a top level aggregation and there aren’t sub-aggregations. Kibana uses Date histogram in this form very very frequently.
