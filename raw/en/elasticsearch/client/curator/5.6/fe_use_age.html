use_ageedit

- filtertype: count
  count: 10
  use_age: True
  source: creation_date

This setting allows filtering of indices by their age after other considerations.

The default value of this setting is False

Use of this setting requires the additional setting, source.

There are context-specific examples using use_age in the count and space documentation.
